Best-Ever Cucumber Dill Salad

Photo of Best-Ever Cucumber Dill Salad

Just experimenting one day and came up with a yummy, fresh twist on an old recipe.

A quick salad made with 3 to 4 ingredients. Can be combined with tacos or rice for better flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cucumber, very thinly sliced
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup sour cream, or to taste
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1/2 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon dill
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Place cucumber slices in a colander. Generously sprinkle salt over cucumber; let sit until water starts to bead on the slices, about 10 minutes. Drain water from cucumber slices.
