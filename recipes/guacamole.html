Guacamole

Photo of Guacamole

Just experimenting one day and came up with a yummy, fresh twist on an old recipe.

A quick side made with 3 to 4 ingredients. Can be combined with tacos or lime rice for better flavor.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup onion, diced

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt.
  2. Mix in onion, cilantro and tomatoes
  3. Stir in cayenne pepper.
  4. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.
